About Lincoln Freight

Lincoln has been helping improve our customers supply chain and transportation process since 1997. Lincoln has established itself as the leading customer service freight broker in North America. We have built our reputation based on less than truck load shipments to/from the US . We are determined to be your freight partner and an integral part of your business. With an emphasis on premium service and communication we strive to become an extension of your operations/traffic team. At Lincoln, you will be never be caught off guard with your shipments. Our knowledgeable and helpful agents ensure you are kept informed regarding the status or your shipment from start to finish.

Honesty isn’t just a part of our name, it’s our service promise.

Why Choose Us

Offering the best Over-The-Road LTL and Truck load service between Canada and the US since 1997
Choosing the right freight partner is essential to any business. At Lincoln we put an emphasis on helping you grow your business through the shipping process
Developing strong relationships with you, your shippers, receivers, consignees and customers is our main goal on each and every shipment.
Everyone involved with your shipments will have an accountable, Lincoln representative to contact to ensure the process is constantly improved.
We have aligned ourselves with the best carriers in the industry to ensure your freight travels in a safe cost effective and timely manner.
We have Experienced and Knowledgeable staff to ensure many of the problems with shipments are avoided all together. In rare cases that issues do occur we have staff available 24/7 to problem solve quickly!
