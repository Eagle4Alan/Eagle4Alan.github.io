Bancheri Bros. Interlocking and Paving Corp. is dedicated to providing our customers with professional design and installation for all your interlocking and paving contractor needs.

Interlocking concrete pavers are a smart and elegant design choice. They are manufactured to withstand weight and weather. They are laid in bedding sand just as flagstone, cobblestone, and brick, but they interlock to provide a perfect, stable fit. They come in a wide variety of colors, shapes, textures, and designs. Interlocking pavers are easy to maintain and they provide an excellent drainage system for your driveway.

Bancheri Bros. Interlocking and Paving Corp. has installed countless driveways, walkways, retaining walls, steps and patios of all shapes and sizes. Each job is customized and designed to suit each and every single site, we offer free on-site consultations and estimates for every job.
